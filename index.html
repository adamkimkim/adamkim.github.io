about

keepitinside

keepitinside is a project where the music is focused on the inner self. Using a mixture of electronic beats and live instruments, the music attempts to combine a mellow background supported by harsh instruments. keepitinside strives to convey that there are dark sides to smiling faces and, sometimes, all you need to do is listen.
